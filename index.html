About

Translating policy ambition into implementable reform

Pharmalith Advisory is a specialist global health and pharmaceutical systems consultancy that supports governments, donors, and global health organisations to translate policy ambition into practical, implementable reforms that improve access to safe, effective, and affordable medical products.

The firm operates with a lean, expert-led model, combining rigorous analysis with hands-on delivery globally, with a focus on Africa.

Vision

A world in which every patient, regardless of geography or income, can trust the safety, quality, and effectiveness of the medicines they receive.

Services

Core service areas

01

Strategic Advisory

Pharmaceutical system and regulatory landscape assessments, regulatory reform roadmaps and implementation planning, market-shaping and access strategies for essential medicines, and policy brief development with legislative drafting support.

02

Technical Assistance

Capacity strengthening for national medicines regulatory authorities, design and delivery of quality-assurance, surveillance, and traceability pilots, procurement and supply-chain optimisation, and pooled procurement feasibility studies.

03

Research & Evidence

Burden and risk modelling for substandard and falsified medical products, cost-effectiveness and economic evaluation of pharmaceutical interventions, political economy and institutional analysis, and evidence synthesis with applied research.
